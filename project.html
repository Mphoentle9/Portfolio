Project Overview

Our team is developing a smart online appointment scheduling and navigation system that makes it simple for students to schedule an appointment with the Faculty of Natural Sciences, Mathematical Sciences and Computing Department at Walter Sisulu University.

This innovative system aims to streamline the appointment booking process, reduce scheduling conflicts, and enhance the overall experience for both students and lecturers.

Project Objectives

  • To automate and simplify the appointment scheduling process between students and lecturers.
  • To improve time management and prevent overlapping or double-booked appointments.
  • To enhance accessibility and convenience for users through a mobile-based platform.
  • To provide real-time availability information for lecturers.
  • To implement a navigation system to help students locate lecturer offices.
  • To generate analytics and reports on appointment patterns and resource utilization.

Project Details

System Features

  • User-friendly interface for students and lecturers
  • Real-time availability tracking
  • Automated appointment reminders
  • Conflict detection and resolution
  • Campus navigation assistance
  • Reporting and analytics dashboard
  • Mobile application for on-the-go access

Expected Outcomes

The Smart Lecturer Scheduling System is expected to significantly reduce scheduling conflicts, decrease administrative workload, improve student-lecturer interactions, and enhance overall efficiency within the department.

Project Timeline

This project is scheduled for completion within 6 months, with planned deployment at the beginning of the next academic semester.
